Our Story

Magnaga Water Beach Resort as the vision of Rey T. Uy, a family passionate about creating unforgettable beach getaways. Nestled on the pristine shores of Pantukan, Philippines, the resort has become a haven for travelers seeking relaxation, adventure, and connection with the beauty of nature.

Mission & Value

At Magnaga Water Beach Resort, we are dedicated to providing exceptional service, comfortable accommodations, and diverse experiences that cater to every guest's needs. We believe in:

  • **Creating lasting memories:** We strive to provide you with a memorable vacation experience that you'll cherish for years to come.
  • **Sustainable practices:** We are committed to responsible tourism and environmental sustainability, preserving the beauty of our surroundings for future generations.
  • **Unwavering hospitality:** Our friendly and dedicated team is committed to exceeding your expectations and ensuring you have a comfortable and enjoyable stay.
